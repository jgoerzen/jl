  • freq:
    For HF: integer frequency in kHz; if freq not known then 28000, 21000, 14000, 7000, 3500, 1800
    For VHF/UHF; numberic band representations as follows: 50 MHz=50, 144 MHz=144, 222 MHz=222, 432 MHz=432, 902 MHz=903, 1296 MHz=1.2, 2304 MHz=2.3, 3456 MHz=3.4, 5760 MHz=5.7, 10 GHz=10, 24 GHz=24, 47 GHz=47, 76 GHz=76, 119 GHz=119, 142 GHz=142, 241 GHz=241, 300 GHz=300, and Light=LIGHT.

  • mo:
    CW, PH, FM or RY (in the case of cross-mode QSOs, indicate the transmitting mode)

  • date:
    UTC date in yyyy-mm-dd form

  • time:
    UTC time in nnnn form

  • call:
    callsign

  • rst:
    RST (not used in ARRL-VHF-JAN, ARRL-VHF-JUN, ARRL-VHF-SEP, and STEW-PERRY)

  • exch:
    contest exchange

  • t:
    transmitter id 0 or 1 (used in the multi-two category)

  • nr:
    Serial number, omitted in NAQP (field must be blank)

  • ex1:
    Name in NA Sprint and NAQP

  • ex2:
    QTH in NA Sprint and NAQP, USA and Canada stations use two letter postal code abbreviation, foreign stations stations use QTH; IOTA reference number in RSGB IOTA contests, of form aa-nnn for island stations or '------' for non-island stations.

  • prec:
    Sweepstakes precedence: A, B, M, Q, S or U.

  • ck
    Two digit Sweepstakes check

  • sec
    ARRL Section abbreviation
